Place your PWA icons in this folder:
- icon-192.png (192x192)
- icon-512.png (512x512)
- maskable-192.png (192x192, maskable)
- maskable-512.png (512x512, maskable)
- apple-touch-icon.png (180x180)
- favicon-16.png (16x16)
- favicon-32.png (32x32)
- favicon-48.png (48x48)

These are referenced by manifest.webmanifest and app metadata. Replace with your branded images.
